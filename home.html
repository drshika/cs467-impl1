Visualizing r/AITA

Story:
Context on the subreddit:

  • Everyday thousands of people come to seek advice on their questionable behavior to find out if they were the assholes or not in a particular scenario.
  • The subreddit then classifies the post into four verdicts: Not The Asshole (NTA), You're The Asshole (YTA), Everyone Sucks Here (ESH), No Assholes Here (NAH).

Main questions:
The main questions we are trying to address through this visualization are:

  1. Are there any commonalities in the different categories of posts in r/AITA?
  2. How does the frequency of certain words affect the verdict for the post?

What we found:

  • Mom was a word that was quite common for all four verdicts but it was particularly high in frequency for the Not The Asshole verdict.
  • The word ‘us’ was used quite commonly when the post authors were classified to be assholes.
